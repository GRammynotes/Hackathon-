Open Innovation Challenge

Choose a problem that matters. Build a technology-driven solution that creates real impact.

Problem Statement

While structured problem statements provide a clear direction, true creativity often emerges when participants are free to explore their own ideas. The Open Innovation challenge invites participants to identify a problem of their choice and develop a technology-driven solution that addresses it in a meaningful way. Teams are encouraged to think beyond the predefined tracks, experimenting with novel concepts, unique use cases, or emerging technologies. The focus is on originality, feasibility, and potential impact, allowing participants to showcase their creativity without restrictions. This category serves as a platform for disruptive ideas that may not fit into traditional problem statements but demonstrate strong potential for real-world application and scalability.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Originality & insight
  • Feasibility & technical execution
  • Impact & scalability
  • Design & user experience

Deliverables

  • Brief problem description & motivation
  • Solution architecture & tech stack
  • Interactive demo or prototype
  • Roadmap with milestones

Resources

Use datasets, APIs, and tools that showcase your strengths. Cite sources and document assumptions clearly.
